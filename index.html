FACILITIES

Esella Hotel & Country Club is well-equipped to provide a comfortable and enjoyable stay for its guests. The hotel features an outdoor pool, fitness center, restaurant, bar, and conference facilities. Guests can also take advantage of free Wi-Fi, room service, 24-hour front desk, and complimentary parking.

WEDDINGS

Esella Hotel & Country Club is an ideal venue for weddings and events, offering both indoor and outdoor spaces for ceremonies and receptions. The hotel provides wedding planning services, catering, and audiovisual equipment. With its beautiful surroundings and experienced staff, Esella Hotel & Country Club can help make any wedding unforgettable.

gallery

A must-see for art lovers, featuring a diverse collection of works by both established and emerging artists. The gallery showcases paintings, sculptures, and other art forms from Uganda and beyond. Guests can also enjoy a drink at the gallery's bar, which offers a relaxed atmosphere to take in the artwork. Guided tours of the gallery are available, providing visitors with insight into the artists and their creations.
